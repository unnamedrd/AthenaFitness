Athena's 90 summer fitness plan

Make the commitment today and we can help you do the rest
Our fitness plan includes the following.

  • Athena Community Access

    Gain access to the Athena Community. Go to local meetups, tag your favorite fitness productions, get recommendations from Athena locals for anything from food to hiking trails

  • Personal Training Session

    We offer virtual and in-person training sessions to accomodate all types of lifestyles and schedules.

  • Meal Plan

    Talk to one of our staff nutritionists and get an individualized gameplan for food

  • Equipment Training Session

    One of our pros will help evaluate all of the equipment you use, everything from your shoes, to the level of weight you're using to ensure that you start with the right foundation and know when to progress.

  • Facilities

    Athena partners with select fitness facilities around the country to give you access to a variety of activities. The goal is to keep you engaged and committed to your workout

  • Technology

    Use the Athena app to keep track of all of your progress, and connect to our community. This is data center where you can track your progress towards goals, set reminders.
